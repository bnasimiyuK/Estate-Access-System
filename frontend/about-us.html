Athi Highway Estate Overview

Athi Highway Estate is a modern residential development in Athi River, approximately 800 meters from Mombasa Road along the Athi River–Kinanie Road. It features elegant maisonette-style homes within a secure, family-friendly community. The estate offers piped water, reliable electricity, and controlled access for enhanced safety.

As part of the rapidly growing Athi River–Kinanie neighborhood, Athi Highway Estate reflects the region’s transformation from rural landscapes into vibrant suburban living — surrounded by new estates, shopping malls, and modern infrastructure.
